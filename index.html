Description

PupPredict helps you stay one step ahead of your puppy’s potty needs with a simple, beautiful daily planner with AI built in. An interactive time dial shows where you are in the day and highlights the next recommended potty window so you always know what is coming next. Log meals, and potty breaks in just a tap, then let PupPredict's AI model learn your puppy’s real routine over time. As you log more events, predictions become more accurate and adapt naturally to your pup’s schedule across the day. Your data stays private and securely saved, making it easy to pick up right where you left off on any device.

Key Features:

  • Built-in AI that learns your puppy’s routine as you log potty breaks and meals
  • Secure sign-in to keep your puppy’s schedule private and synced between phones
  • Interactive potty dial with 12-hour time markers, live “now” indicator, and day-to-night gradient
  • Clear next potty window shown directly inside the dial
  • Daily wake and sleep schedule controls to keep routines consistent
  • Fast logging for potty breaks and meals, including backdated entries
  • Activity log with day chips and easy swipe-to-delete actions

#PupPredict Pro: Unlock detailed prediction insights and the full estimates dashboard while keeping all core logging tools free. Upgrading or restoring your purchase is quick and seamless, so you can focus on training with confidence.
