Privacy Policy — PupPredict Last updated: 12/22/2025

  1. Overview PupPredict helps pet owners log potty breaks, meals, and daily routines, and view predictive potty reminders through an interactive planner. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure.
  2. Information We Collect Account Information When you create an account, we collect your email address and secure sign-in credentials to authenticate access to your data. Usage and Activity Data We collect the information you choose to log, such as potty events, meals, wake and sleep times, and related timestamps. This data is used to provide predictions and scheduling features. Purchase Information If you purchase PupPredict Pro, we receive confirmation of your purchase status from the App Store to unlock premium features. We do not receive your payment or billing details. Device and Diagnostics Data We may receive limited, non-identifying diagnostic information from your device or the App Store to help ensure app stability and validate purchases. We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  3. How We Use Your Information To authenticate your account and keep your data private To provide core app features, including predictions, schedules, and activity logs To enable and restore PupPredict Pro features To improve prediction accuracy as you log more events
  4. Data Storage and Security Your data is stored securely using industry-standard protections. Access to your planner is restricted to you through account sign-in. We retain your activity data while your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time.
  5. Sharing and Third Parties We do not sell or rent your personal data. We use trusted service providers to support app functionality, such as secure data storage and App Store purchase processing. These providers only process data necessary to deliver core features. We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.
  6. Purchases and Subscriptions PupPredict Pro purchases are processed and managed by Apple through the App Store. Purchase restoration and subscription management are handled in accordance with Apple’s policies.
  7. Your Choices You may sign in or out at any time You may request deletion of your account and associated data You can manage or restore purchases through App Store settings
  8. Children’s Privacy PupPredict is not intended for children under 13, or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. Do not use the app without parental consent if you are under the applicable age.
  9. International Users Your data may be processed in regions where our service providers operate. By using the app, you consent to this processing.
